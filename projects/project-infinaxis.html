Infinaxis Data Centre

Overview of Infinaxis Data Centre:

The Infinaxis Data Centre is a cutting-edge digital infrastructure project under construction in Cyberjaya, Malaysia. Developed by Infinaxis Data Centre Platform—a joint venture between Gaw Capital Partners and A3 Capital—the facility spans over 17,000 square meters and is designed to deliver up to 12 megawatts of IT load. Scheduled for completion in Q2 2025, this Tier III-certified data centre will house 10 data halls and accommodate up to 1,830 racks. Strategically located to serve hyperscalers, cloud service providers, and AI workloads, the project marks a significant step in Malaysia's ambition to become a leading digital hub in Southeast Asia.

Project Details

  • Location: Cyberjaya, Malaysia
  • Duration: April 2023 - Augest 2024
  • Role: Senior BIM Modeler
  • LOD: 300
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, Navisworks, AutoCAD
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Independently modeled roads, road furniture, drainage, and water supply systems using Revit.
  • Developed parametric Revit families for drainage and road furniture, ensuring compliance with Building Execution Plans (BEP).
  • Conducted clash detection using Navisworks to identify and resolve conflicts between MEP and structural models.

Challenges and Solutions

The high precision required for modeling infrastructure around the building, including MEP systems, presented significant challenges, particularly in resolving clashes with architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines. Working independently, I implemented rigorous quality checks and utilized Navisworks for real-time clash detection and resolution, ensuring model accuracy and seamless coordination across all disciplines.

Outcomes

The BIM models facilitated precise installation of critical systems, supporting the data centre’s operational reliability and meeting project timelines.
