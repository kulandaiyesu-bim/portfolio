MRCB's Mukim Setapak

Overview of MRCB's Mukim Setapak:

MRCB's Mukim Setapak project is a utility-focused development in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, aimed at enhancing the urban infrastructure with advanced utility networks for water supply, sewage, and drainage systems to support residential and commercial growth in the area.

Project Details

  • Location: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
  • Duration: Jun 2024 - Jul 2024
  • LOD: 300
  • Role: BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, Civil 3D, Navisworks, AutoCAD
  • Reference: Project Reference Link

Key Contributions

  • Modelled road, drainage, electrical, and telecom services around the building in Revit.
  • Created custom parametric families for drainage and road furniture in Revit.
  • Resolved internal and interdisciplinary clashes around the building to ensure a clash-free model.

Challenges and Solutions

Modeling road, drainage, electrical, and telecom services around a building in a dense urban setting posed challenges due to spatial constraints and interdisciplinary coordination. Creating custom parametric families for drainage and road furniture required precision to meet project specifications. I addressed these by developing accurate Revit models and custom families, ensuring compliance with BIM Execution Plan (BEP) standards. Regular clash detection using Navisworks allowed me to identify and resolve conflicts with other disciplines, such as structural and MEP systems, through iterative coordination and model adjustments.

Outcomes

Delivered a clash-free BIM model for road, drainage, electrical, and telecom services, enhancing the project's infrastructure reliability. The custom parametric families for drainage and road furniture improved modeling efficiency and consistency, supporting accurate documentation and construction planning. By resolving internal and interdisciplinary clashes using Navisworks, I ensured seamless integration with other building systems, contributing to the project's progress and stakeholder satisfaction.
