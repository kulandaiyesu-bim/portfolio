Kwasa Damansara

Overview of Kwasa Damansara:

Kwasa Damansara is a large-scale township development in Selangor, Malaysia, integrating residential, commercial, and infrastructure elements. The project includes advanced utility and infrastructure systems to support sustainable urban growth and connectivity.

Project Details

  • Location: Selangor, Malaysia
  • Duration: Mar 2024 - Ongoing
  • LOD: 300
  • Role: BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, Civil 3D, Navisworks, Dynamo (basic usage), AutoCAD
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Modeled wet and dry utility networks, including water supply, sewage, drainage, and electrical systems, across key township areas.
  • Used Dynamo in Revit for automating repetitive tasks, such as utility component placement, to enhance efficiency.
  • Ensured compliance with BIM Execution Plan (BEP) standards for seamless data integration and IFC compliance.
  • Conducted clash detection using Navisworks to resolve conflicts with structural and architectural models.

Challenges and Solutions

The complexity of integrating utility and infrastructure models in a large-scale township required precise coordination. By conducting regular clash detection with Navisworks and adhering to BEP standards, I ensured accurate model integration. Automation with Dynamo reduced manual modeling time, while frequent stakeholder coordination meetings helped align all disciplines effectively.

Outcomes

Delivered high-quality, clash-free utility and infrastructure models that supported the township's development goals. The use of Dynamo improved workflow efficiency, and Navisworks ensured seamless integration with other disciplines. My contributions facilitated reliable and sustainable infrastructure systems, enhancing the project's overall progress.
