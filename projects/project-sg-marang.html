Sungai Marang Bridge Upgrade

Overview of the Project:

The Sungai Marang Bridge Upgrade is a major infrastructure project located in Marang, Terengganu, Malaysia. Initiated in December 2021 and scheduled for completion by October 2025, the RM338.5 million project aims to replace the aging 1960s bridge with a new 900-meter dual-lane structure. This upgrade will improve traffic flow along Federal Route FT003, enhance connectivity between Marang and Kuala Terengganu, and support the movement of larger fishing vessels. The project also plays a vital role in boosting local economic activity, especially in the fishing and tourism sectors.

Project Details

  • Location: Terengganu, Malaysia
  • Duration: Apr 2023 - Sep 2024
  • Role: Junior BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd., Chennai.
  • Tools Used: Civil 3D, Revit, Navisworks, Dynamo
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Created parametric Revit families for bridge components, ensuring compliance with BEP standards.
  • Modeled structural and utility networks, including drainage and electrical systems, using Civil 3D.
  • Performed clash detection with Navisworks to resolve conflicts between structural and utility models.
  • Used Dynamo for basic automation tasks like placing multiple families and improving efficiency.

Challenges and Solutions

One major challenge was integrating legacy design data with modern BIM workflows. I used standard conversion tools and manual adjustments to ensure compatibility, enabling seamless model integration. Coordinating with international teams required precise IFC-compliant models, which I achieved through rigorous quality checks.

Outcomes

The project was delivered on time, with the BIM models facilitating efficient construction planning and reducing on-site conflicts. The upgraded bridge now supports higher traffic volumes and improved safety standards.
